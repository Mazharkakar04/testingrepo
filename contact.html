Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include completed application form, previous school records, birth certificate, and an entrance assessment. Please contact our admissions office for detailed information.

What are the school fees?

School fees vary by grade level and include tuition, books, and other educational materials. We offer flexible payment plans and scholarships for eligible students.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services to various areas. Our school buses are equipped with modern safety features and are operated by trained drivers.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, art, drama, debate, science clubs, and community service programs.
