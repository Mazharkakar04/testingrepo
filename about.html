About Us

Discover the rich history and values that make Iqra Public High School a beacon of educational excellence

Our History

Founded in 2001, Iqra Public High School & Junior College Mohadi began as a small educational institution with a vision to provide quality education to the local community. What started with just 50 students and 5 teachers has now grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Over the past 25 years, our school has witnessed remarkable growth and transformation. We have expanded our facilities, enhanced our curriculum, and built a strong reputation for academic excellence and character development. Our commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals has remained constant throughout our journey.

Today, Iqra Public High School stands as a testament to the power of dedicated education, serving over 500 students from diverse backgrounds and preparing them for successful futures in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

Empowering students to learn, grow, and make a positive difference in the world.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, high-quality education that fosters academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills in a nurturing and inclusive environment that respects diversity and promotes lifelong learning.
