Our Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities designed to provide the best learning environment for our students

World-Class Learning Environment

At Iqra Public High School, we believe that excellent facilities are essential for providing quality education. Our campus is equipped with modern, well-maintained facilities that support both academic excellence and personal development. From cutting-edge technology labs to spacious sports grounds, every facility is designed to enhance the learning experience and foster creativity, innovation, and growth.

Library

Our modern library houses over 10,000 books, digital resources, and study spaces. Students have access to a wide range of literature, reference materials, and online databases to support their research and reading needs.

  • 10,000+ Books
  • Digital Resources
  • Quiet Study Areas
  • Computer Stations

Computer Lab

Our fully-equipped computer laboratory features the latest technology with high-speed internet connectivity. Students learn programming, digital skills, and use educational software in a modern computing environment.

  • Latest Computers
  • High-Speed Internet
  • Programming Software
  • Multimedia Tools

Science Labs

Our well-equipped physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories provide hands-on learning experiences. Students conduct experiments using modern equipment and safety protocols under expert supervision.

  • Physics Lab
  • Chemistry Lab
  • Biology Lab
  • Safety Equipment

Sports Ground

Our extensive sports facilities include football fields, basketball courts, tennis courts, and athletic tracks. These facilities promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students.

  • Football Field
  • Basketball Courts
  • Tennis Courts
  • Athletic Track
